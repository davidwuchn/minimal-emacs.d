# OV5.1 Consolidated

Research findings are now handled by the OV5 research pipeline:
- **Research coordinator**: ontology × AutoTTS × AutoGo → category-specific priorities
- **Findings injection**: strips `<think>` blocks, extracts actionable patterns, max 500 chars
- **Mementum storage**: insights stored as atomic memories, synthesized into knowledge pages
