# Harvey vs LegalOS — Enterprise Legal AI Gap Analysis

**Source:** [harvey.ai](https://harvey.ai) — enterprise legal AI platform. 142K+ professionals, 60+ AmLaw 100 firms, 65 countries, SOC2/GDPR/ISO certified. Sequoia/OpenAI backed.

Harvey is the undisputed enterprise leader. LegalOS cannot compete at that tier — it targets a different market entirely.

## 1. Harvey Product Suite

| Product | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Assistant** | Ask questions, analyze documents, draft with domain-specific AI |
| **Vault** | Securely store, organize, bulk-analyze legal documents |
| **Knowledge** | Research complex legal, regulatory, and tax questions |
| **Agents** | Purpose-built agents execute legal work end-to-end |
| **Contract Intelligence** | Surface insights, strengthen negotiations, accelerate reviews |
| **Command Center** | Analytics, benchmarking, agentic insights |
| **Harvey Mobile** | iOS/Android access |
| **Ecosystem** | Integrates with existing tools |
| **Shared Spaces** | Cross-organization collaboration |

## 2. Market Positioning

| | Harvey | LegalOS |
|---|---|---|
| **Target** | AmLaw 100, Fortune 500 in-house | Solo lawyers, small firms (<50 attorneys) |

## 4. LegalOS's Advantages (Harvey Can't Match)

| LegalOS | Harvey Gap |
|---------|-----------|
| Self-improving (experiments compound accuracy) | Harvey is static between releases |
| 12-backend auto-failover routing | Harvey likely uses single provider per feature |
| $200/mo total operating cost | Harvey has massive infrastructure costs |
| Git-immutable Datahike (full audit trail) | Harvey uses standard DB |
| Open source / self-hostable | Harvey is proprietary SaaS |
| OV5 experiment loop improves all modules daily | Manual improvement cycles |
| Mementum remembers every case analysis | Stateless between sessions |
| 1 person operation (15 min/day review) | Harvey has large engineering team |

## 5. Honest Assessment

**LegalOS cannot compete with Harvey at the enterprise level.** The certification, compliance, integration, and trust requirements are insurmountable for a solo developer.

**LegalOS CAN compete in the market Harvey ignores:** solo attorneys, small firms (1-10 lawyers), and legal aid organizations who cannot afford $1K+/seat/month for Harvey but still need AI document analysis, case research, and contract review.

**The niche:** Self-improving legal AI for the 1.2 million US lawyers NOT at AmLaw 100 firms. These lawyers currently use Microsoft Word, Google, and manual research. They need basic AI assistance at an affordable price. Harvey does not serve them. LegalOS can.
