In `gptel-benchmark--eight-keys-score-with-weights`, wrap the score computation in `ignore-errors` + fallback to 0.0. This prevents a single malformed key definition (e.g., missing :signals/:anti-patterns property causing `plist-get` on non-plist) from crashing the entire scoring loop. The `unless score (setq score 0.0)` ensures predictable fallback instead of silently skipping the key.
